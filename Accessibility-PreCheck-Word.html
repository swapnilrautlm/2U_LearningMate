Checks are based on the Learning Accessibility Guidebook and WCAG 2.0 / PDF-UA best practice. Word files are checked against "Preparing a Word document" (p.16); PDFs are checked for structural accessibility essentials. This tool flags likely issues for human review; it does not replace Acrobat's full accessibility check (e.g. colour contrast, reading-order correctness).
